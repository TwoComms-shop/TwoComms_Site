1. What data this covers

DIRECT_BOT may process Instagram Direct customer support data, including Instagram sender identifiers, username, display name, messages sent to twocomms, attachments, order-support details voluntarily provided by the user, conversation stage, bot logs, and operational error logs.

We do not sell Instagram Direct message data. We use it to answer customer questions, help with clothing selection and sizing, and support order preparation.

2. Remove app access in Meta / Instagram

If you connected or interacted with DIRECT_BOT through Meta technologies and want to remove app access, you can use Meta's app and website settings:

  1. Open your Facebook or Instagram account settings.
  2. Go to "Apps and Websites" or "Business Integrations".
  3. Find DIRECT_BOT or the connected twocomms integration.
  4. Select "Remove". If Meta offers "Request data deletion", submit the request there as well.
Removing app access in Meta stops future access where Meta supports that control. It does not always remove order records or support records already stored by twocomms, so use the email request below if you want us to delete eligible records from our systems.

3. Request deletion from twocomms

You can submit a deletion request directly on this page. Enter the Instagram username, Instagram profile URL, or Instagram sender ID used in the conversation with @twocomms.

{% csrf_token %} We only process exact matching DIRECT_BOT records. If no matching records are found, you will still receive a confirmation page.

You may also email cooperation@twocomms.shop with the subject "DIRECT_BOT data deletion request" and include:

  • your Instagram username used to contact @twocomms;
  • the approximate date of your conversation, if you remember it;
  • any phone number or order number only if your request is related to an order;
  • a short request such as "Please delete my DIRECT_BOT Instagram Direct data".

We may ask for limited verification information to confirm that the request relates to the correct Instagram conversation or order. After verification, we will delete or anonymize eligible bot conversation records unless we must keep limited data for legal, tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, delivery, payment, or dispute-resolution obligations.

4. What happens after a request

  1. We review the request and identify matching DIRECT_BOT records.
  2. We delete or anonymize eligible conversation and bot records.
  3. We keep only data that is legally required or necessary for order/payment/dispute records, and only for the required period.
  4. We reply with confirmation when the request is completed or explain why a limited record cannot be deleted immediately.
