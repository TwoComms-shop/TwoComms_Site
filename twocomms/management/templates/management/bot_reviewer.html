What reviewers can verify

DIRECT_BOT helps customers who message the official @twocomms Instagram account. It answers clothing support questions about availability, sizing, colors, delivery, payment, order preparation, and manager handoff.

  • The bot uses Instagram Direct messaging only for customer support conversations.
  • Replies may be generated with Google Gemini Flash AI using twocomms support rules.
  • A human TwoComms manager can review or take over support conversations when needed.
  • The app does not post on behalf of users and does not use data for advertising resale or unrelated profiling.
