Privacy Policy for the Tucoms Instagram Direct Bot

This policy explains how Tucoms, operated by TwoComms, processes information received through Instagram Direct messages on the official Instagram account instagram.com/twocomms when customers ask about clothing, product availability, sizing, delivery, payment, or order support.

1. What the bot does

The Tucoms Instagram Direct Bot is the Meta application named DIRECT_BOT (App ID 2120980214971807). It helps people who contact the official Tucoms Instagram account at https://www.instagram.com/twocomms/. It replies to customer questions, helps select clothing sizes and models, explains product availability, shares order and delivery guidance, and may help prepare an order request for a human manager.

Customer replies may be drafted with Google Gemini Flash AI technology, currently Gemini 3.1 Flash or another compatible Gemini Flash model configured for the same support purpose. The bot does not provide unrelated services, does not post on behalf of users, does not access private Instagram content outside the Direct conversation with Tucoms, and does not sell personal data.

2. Information we process

Depending on what a customer sends to Tucoms in Instagram Direct, we may process:

  • Instagram account identifiers needed to receive and reply to the message, such as the sender ID, username, display name, and profile picture URL if provided by Meta.
  • Message content sent to Tucoms, including text, product questions, sizing preferences, photos, story replies, referral data, and attachments.
  • Order support information that the customer chooses to provide, such as name, phone number, preferred item, size, color, quantity, delivery city, Nova Poshta details, payment status, and order comments.
  • Technical and operational data needed to run the service, such as message timestamps, bot status, delivery errors from Meta, moderation flags, and conversation stage.

3. How we use the information

We use the information only for customer communication and order support:

  • to answer Instagram Direct messages from customers;
  • to recommend relevant products, sizes, availability, prices, delivery options, and payment steps;
  • to create or update an order or hand the conversation to a Tucoms manager;
  • to prevent spam, abuse, duplicate requests, and operational errors;
  • to maintain logs needed for customer support, security, and troubleshooting of Meta API access.

4. Legal basis

We process Direct message data because the customer starts or continues a conversation with Tucoms and expects a response. Where applicable, the legal bases are performance of a customer request or order, legitimate interest in providing customer support and preventing abuse, and consent where the customer voluntarily provides optional information.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal data. We do not share Instagram Direct message data with advertising data brokers.

Information may be processed by service providers only when needed to operate customer support and order fulfillment, including:

  • Meta / Instagram APIs for receiving and sending Direct messages;
  • hosting, database, security, and logging providers used to run the TwoComms website and management system;
  • Google Gemini / compatible Gemini Flash AI services used to draft customer support replies, limited to the conversation context needed for the reply;
  • delivery, payment, accounting, or order-management providers when the customer asks to place or complete an order;
  • authorized Tucoms / TwoComms managers who handle customer conversations and orders.

6. Data retention

We keep conversation and order-support records only for as long as needed to provide support, fulfill orders, resolve disputes, maintain security, and meet legal or accounting obligations. Bot diagnostic logs may be kept for troubleshooting and then deleted or minimized when they are no longer needed.

If a customer requests deletion, we will delete or anonymize eligible Direct message and bot records unless we must keep limited information for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution reasons.

7. User rights and data deletion requests

Customers may ask us to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of their personal data. To request data deletion, send an email to cooperation@twocomms.shop with:

  • the Instagram username used to contact Tucoms;
  • a short request such as "delete my Instagram Direct bot data";
  • any order number or phone number if the request is related to an order.

We may ask for limited information to verify that the request relates to the correct Instagram conversation or order.

8. Security

We use access controls, HTTPS, account permissions, operational logging, and restricted manager access to protect customer information. Only authorized staff can access the management tools used for Instagram customer support.

9. Children

The bot is intended for customers who can lawfully purchase clothing or communicate with a business about an order. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate deletion steps.

10. International processing

Tucoms / TwoComms operates from Ukraine and may use infrastructure or service providers located in other countries. When information is transferred or processed internationally, we use it only for the purposes described in this policy and apply reasonable safeguards.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our bot, order workflow, legal obligations, or service providers change. The latest version will always be available at this public URL.
