The bot may answer questions about clothing, product availability, sizing, prices, delivery, payment, returns, and order preparation. Replies may be drafted with Google Gemini Flash AI, currently Gemini 3.1 Flash or a compatible Gemini Flash model.

The service is intended only for customer support and order assistance for the official twocomms Instagram account at https://www.instagram.com/twocomms/.
